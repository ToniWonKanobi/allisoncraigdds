Welcome

Dr. Allison Craig attended dental school in Southern California at Loma Linda University School of Dentistry. She graduated in the top 10 of her class, and was inducted in to the Omicron Kappa Upsilon National Dental Honor Society. While in dental school, she participated in clinics and service projects for the undeserved.

After graduating, Dr. Craig completed an Advanced Education in General Dentistry (AEGD) through NYU Lutheran Dental Medicine.
